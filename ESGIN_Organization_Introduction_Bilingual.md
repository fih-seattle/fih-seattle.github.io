## Who We Are
## 組織定位

The Global ESG Leadership Organization (ESGIN) is an international, implementation-oriented sustainability organization registered in the United Kingdom, headquartered in **Seattle, USA**. Operating across more than **60 countries and territories**, ESGIN functions as a global multi-stakeholder platform connecting business leaders, sustainability practitioners, policymakers, financial institutions, academia, youth leaders, women leaders, and civil society actors to accelerate the implementation of the **2030 Agenda for Sustainable Development**.

全球永續領導組織（ESGIN），即世界永續領袖峰會，是一個在英國正式註冊、總部設於**美國西雅圖**的國際性、以實踐為導向的永續發展組織，業務遍及逾 **60 個國家與地區**。ESGIN 作為全球多利益相關方平台，連結商業領袖、永續發展實踐者、政策制定者、金融機構、學術界、青年領袖、女性領袖與公民社會，共同加速推動 **2030 永續發展議程**的落地實踐。

Distinct from organizations focused primarily on policy advocacy or awareness-building, ESGIN emphasizes measurable implementation and institutional capacity-building. The organization develops practical pathways that help enterprises, institutions, and communities operationalize sustainability commitments through implementation-oriented collaboration, practitioner-informed learning, governance frameworks, leadership programmes, sustainability education, and ecosystem partnerships.

有別於以政策倡議或意識建構為主的組織，ESGIN 著重可衡量的落地實踐與機構能力建設。組織透過以實踐為導向的協作、從業者知識學習、治理框架、領導力計劃、永續教育與生態系統夥伴關係，為企業、機構與社區提供將永續承諾轉化為行動的切實路徑。

As a participating organization of the **UN Global Compact** (since May 2025) and a signatory to the **UN-supported Principles for Responsible Investment (PRI)** (since 2024), ESGIN promotes responsible business conduct, ethical governance, sustainability integration, institutional accountability, anti-greenwashing principles, and long-term societal value creation.

作為**聯合國全球契約**的正式參與組織（自 2025 年 5 月起）及**聯合國責任投資原則（PRI）**的簽署方（自 2024 年起），ESGIN 倡導負責任的商業行為、道德治理、永續整合、機構問責、反漂綠原則與長期社會價值創造。

## Mission
## 使命

ESGIN advances sustainable and inclusive development by translating ESG and SDG commitments into practical implementation pathways. The organization mobilizes leadership, partnerships, practitioner expertise, sustainability education, and cross-sector collaboration to strengthen implementation capacity across institutions, enterprises, and communities.

ESGIN 致力於將 ESG 與 SDG 承諾轉化為切實可行的落地路徑，推動永續與包容性發展。組織動員領導力、夥伴關係、實踐者專業知識、永續教育與跨部門協作，強化機構、企業與社區的落地執行能力。

ESGIN seeks to bridge the gap between sustainability ambition and measurable delivery by supporting responsible governance, inclusive participation, ethical innovation, and scalable implementation ecosystems aligned with the **2030 Agenda for Sustainable Development**.

ESGIN 致力於彌合永續發展願景與可衡量成果之間的鴻溝，透過負責任的治理、包容性參與、道德創新與可擴展的落地生態系統，與 **2030 永續發展議程**深度對齊。

Guided by five core principles — **Integrity · Inclusivity · Intelligence · Innovation & Technology · Initiative** — ESGIN applies a "Glo-Cal" model that links localized implementation with global collaboration and knowledge exchange.

秉持五大核心原則——**誠信 · 包容 · 智識 · 創新與科技 · 行動力**——ESGIN 採用「全球在地化（Glo-Cal）」模式，將在地落地實踐與全球協作及知識交流緊密連結。

## What We Do: Four Infrastructure Pillars
## 行動體系：四大基礎設施支柱

ESGIN's purpose is structured around four mutually reinforcing infrastructure pillars:

ESGIN 的行動體系由四大相互強化的基礎設施支柱構成：

### I. Leadership Infrastructure
### 一、領導力基礎設施

ESGIN strengthens ESG and sustainability capabilities among SME owners, professionals, women, youth, and emerging leaders through structured education programmes, fellowships, and applied learning initiatives aligned with internationally recognized sustainability frameworks — including women's leadership, climate action, responsible digital transformation, and youth leadership.

ESGIN 透過與國際認可永續框架對齊的系統性教育課程、獎學金計劃與應用學習項目，強化中小企業主、專業人士、女性、青年及新興領袖的 ESG 與永續能力，涵蓋女性領導力、氣候行動、負責任數位轉型與青年領導力。

*2025–2026: 1,200+ participants across Hong Kong, Taiwan and Thailand.*

*2025 至 2026 年：逾 1,200 名學員遍及香港、台灣及泰國。*

### II. Intelligence Infrastructure
### 二、智識基礎設施

ESGIN produces and disseminates applied sustainability knowledge through **ESG in Action Monthly** (ISSN 2979-0972) — capturing real-world implementation practices and translating them into replicable approaches for enterprises, policymakers, and communities. The publication is permanently archived across **all 6 UK & Ireland Legal Deposit Libraries**.

ESGIN 透過《**ESG in Action Monthly**》（ISSN 2979-0972）生產並傳播應用型永續知識，記錄真實世界的落地實踐，將其轉化為企業、政策制定者與社區可複製的行動方案。本刊物已永久收藏於**英國及愛爾蘭全部 6 所法定呈繳圖書館**。

### III. Policy Infrastructure
### 三、政策基礎設施

ESGIN develops and contributes practical solutions through submissions to the **UN DESA Doha Solutions Platform for Social Development**, formal policy papers, and stakeholder consultations — addressing responsible digital governance, women's economic participation, inclusive finance, and responsible AI adoption.

ESGIN 透過向**聯合國多哈社會發展解決方案平台**提交倡議、發布正式政策論文及參與利益相關方磋商，貢獻切實可行的解決方案，聚焦負責任數位治理、女性經濟參與、普惠金融及負責任人工智能應用。

*11 initiatives submitted. 8 formally accepted and published.*

*提交 11 項倡議，其中 8 項已獲正式接受並發布。*

### IV. Action Infrastructure
### 四、行動基礎設施

ESGIN convenes a global network of more than **6,000 participants** and over **350 senior advisors and contributors** across more than **60 countries and territories** through convenings, regional chapters, peer learning platforms, cross-sector dialogues, and the **Future Intelligence Hub (FIH)** — ESGIN's flagship urban innovation platform.

ESGIN 匯聚遍及逾 **60 個國家與地區**、超過 **6,000 名參與者** 及逾 **350 位資深顧問與貢獻者** 的全球網絡，透過論壇峰會、區域分會、同儕學習平台、跨部門對話，以及 ESGIN 旗艦城市創新平台**未來智慧中心（FIH）**，建立協調一致的 ESG 與 SDG 落地路徑。

We are currently the only platform dedicated to ESG practical implementation, going far beyond compliance and data reporting. Our goal is to help global leaders and organisations not only "understand ESG" but truly "do ESG" and generate measurable change.

我們目前是唯一一個專注於 ESG 實踐落地的平台，遠遠超越合規要求與數據報告的範疇。我們的目標是幫助全球領導者和組織不僅「了解 ESG」，更能真正「踐行 ESG」並創造可量化的改變。

### ESG in Action Monthly | ESG 行動月刊

ESG in Action Monthly is the industry's first and only professional journal dedicated exclusively to ESG practice and implementation. While the broader market continues to focus primarily on compliance metrics and data disclosure, many organisations still face significant challenges in translating policy into effective decision-making and tangible outcomes.

《ESG in Action Monthly》是業界首本、也是唯一一本專注於 ESG 實踐與落地的專業期刊。當主流市場仍主要聚焦於合規指標與數據披露時，許多組織仍面臨如何將政策轉化為有效決策與具體成果的重大挑戰。

We transform complex topics — including human rights due diligence, climate transition, nature-positive finance, and the UN Doha 8 Initiatives — into clear, immediately applicable content. Every article includes real case studies, implementation challenges, honest data, and specific next-step recommendations. We always present both successes and difficulties to avoid any form of greenwashing.

我們將人權盡職調查、氣候轉型、自然正向金融及多哈八大倡議等複雜議題，轉化為清晰且可立即應用的內容。每篇文章均包含真實案例、執行挑戰、誠實數據及具體的後續行動建議，並始終呈現成功與困難兩面，以避免任何形式的漂綠（greenwashing）。

All content is rigorously fact-checked by our network of over **350 international advisors** and aligned with UN and PRI sources.

所有內容均經逾 **350 位國際顧問**的嚴格事實核查，並與聯合國及 PRI 來源保持一致。

## Platform Architecture
## 平台架構

ESGIN operates through a dual-track institutional approach combining policy advocacy with implementation infrastructure.

ESGIN 採用雙軌並行的機構運作模式，將政策倡導與落地實踐基礎設施有機結合。

**Track 1 — Policy & Multilateral Engagement 第一軌 — 政策與多邊參與**
Contributing practitioner-informed perspectives to multilateral dialogue, UN processes, intergovernmental forums, and global governance consultations.
向多邊對話、聯合國進程、政府間論壇及全球治理磋商貢獻來自實踐者的洞察與視角。

**Track 2 — Implementation Infrastructure 第二軌 — 落地實踐基礎設施**
Building the operational mechanisms that support measurable and locally adaptable sustainability action — through industry councils, regional hubs, education programmes, fellowships, and the Future Intelligence Hub city innovation platform.
構建支撐可衡量、在地化永續行動的運作機制——透過產業理事會、區域中心、教育課程、獎學金計劃及未來智慧中心城市創新平台。

## Future Intelligence Hub (FIH) — ESGIN's Flagship Implementation Platform
## 未來智慧中心（FIH）— ESGIN 旗艦落地平台

FIH is ESGIN's flagship urban innovation infrastructure — a physical, AI-powered social innovation and micro-entrepreneurship incubation hub empowering sustainable urban and community development.

未來智慧中心（FIH）是 ESGIN 的旗艦城市創新基礎設施——一個以 AI 驅動社會創新與微型創業孵化的實體空間，賦能永續城市與社區發展。

**"Connecting People, Knowledge, Resources and Solutions to Build the Future Society — Powered by AI."**

**「以 AI 驅動人才、知識、資源與解方的連結，共同建構未來社會。」**

FIH begins from a gap: the opportunities created by AI are expanding faster than the speed at which people can gain access to them — particularly micro-entrepreneurs and local innovators who are excluded from capital, mentorship, and digital tools. FIH establishes a physical, on-the-ground space in every city where it operates, turning ESGIN's global initiatives into city-level action through micro-entrepreneurship incubation, AI capability building, SDG-aligned innovation, and Opportunity Day capital connections.

FIH 的起點是一個落差：AI 帶來的機會正在快速擴張，但能力、資源與舞台的取得速度遠遠跟不上——尤其是被排除在資本、導師與數位工具之外的微型創業者與在地創新者。FIH 在每一座落地城市建立一座實體進駐空間，透過微型創業孵化、AI 能力建設、SDG 對齊的創新設計及 Opportunity Day 資本連結，將 ESGIN 的全球倡議轉化為城市層面的具體行動。

The model: **Global Host (ESGIN) + Local Implementation Partner** — asset-light, globally standardized, locally executed.

運作模式：**全球主辦方（ESGIN）+ 在地執行夥伴**——輕資產、全球標準化、在地執行。

FIH currently supports the following Doha Solutions Platform initiatives: ID60 · ID62 · ID85 · ID87 · ID88 · ID175 · RDY

FIH 目前支持以下多哈解決方案平台倡議：ID60 · ID62 · ID85 · ID87 · ID88 · ID175 · RDY

Launch cities: **Bangkok · Hong Kong · Suzhou · Shanghai · Dubai · London · Taipei · Manila**

首發城市：**曼谷 · 香港 · 蘇州 · 上海 · 杜拜 · 倫敦 · 台北 · 馬尼拉**
